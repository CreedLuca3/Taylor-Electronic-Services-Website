About TES

30 years of making Ontario sound & look its best

Taylor Electronic Services Inc. (TES) is a Canadian company based in Owen Sound, serving a wide area across Southwestern Ontario and beyond. Since 1995 we've provided consultation, design, sales, installation and on-site service of professional audio, video, lighting, surveillance and IT systems.

We've installed systems in houses of worship, arenas, schools, council chambers, training facilities and industrial complexes, and we stand behind and service everything we sell. Whether you do it for fun or for a living, TES is here to make you sound good.
